Plastic Alternative Ideas

It is the right time for us to react and start fighting against pollution and most important thing involved in it is plastic. Here are few things which will help avoid plastic and use some alternative instead of plastic:

  • While going for shopping use your own bags like the handmade bags, jute bags, khadi cotton bags etc. Not only you encourage others to use these bags.
  • Stop buying the bottled water that is available in the market instead take the water in the reusable bottles from home.
  • Opt for paper bags in the malls instead of the plastic bags.
  • If you are storing your food in the plastic wraps or plastic containers. Due to the heat of the food items, it reacts with the plastic and releases harmful chemicals. Which will affect your health if you are going to consume it! Instead, use steel containers or glass containers.
  • Microplastic is the dangerous form of plastic that can‚Äôt be filtered out and which are majorly found in the cosmetics like face wash and toothpaste. So, try to avoid the ‚Äúpolyethylene‚Äù and ‚Äúpolypropylene‚Äù items instead use the other alternatives to it.
  • Stop using the single time use plastic razors and start using the blade changeable razors or the straight razors.
  • Still, if you have some plastic products in home try to recycle those things as much as possible. And try to use objects other than plastic in your daily life.

I Give Up Plastic

Not just the plastic we also should take a pledge to start saving the plants and trees. From the surveys conducted every year, we can easily conclude that the population of the country is increasing every year and it has increased 17.1% in the last 5 years. So, it has become very crucial to plant trees not only in the environment day but on all those days which you think will be giving your future generation a greener, better and healthy life.
